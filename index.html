About Me

Spatial Intelligence,
Applied.

I am a GIS and Remote Sensing Specialist with over 9 years of professional experience in geospatial consultancy. Currently active at PT. Datem Indonesia, PT. Global Asia Teknologi Solusindo, and PT. Barata Geospatial Technology, primarily serving government sector clients across Indonesia.

My expertise spans spatial analysis & modeling, geodatabase development, and satellite imagery interpretation. I work across multiple geospatial platforms including ArcGIS, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, Python, and various other mapping software.

I regularly serve as Adjunct Lecturer in Geomatics Engineering at Universitas Jenderal Achmad Yani (Unjani) and as technical trainer and speaker for government GIS & remote sensing programs.

🏅 Ahli Madya Sistem Informasi Geografis — BNSP Certified (Sep 2024 – Sep 2029)
